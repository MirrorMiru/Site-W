THE KENOVIANS

Kentre

The Kentre (ken-treh) are the dominant subrace of Kenovians, with a higher population and larger influence. The Kentre population as of Sammy's birth was 9 billion with only about 1 billion living of Kenova with the rest living on Kaar'ka. That number would stay relativley stagnant even after Sammy's death and far into the future. The Kentre are native to "the twilight zone" and have developed an adavnaced technological society. The modern Kentre has orange-ish skin, bright blonde hair, no hair anywhere but their head, claws (that they often cut) and the Males are 5 feet tall while females are 7 feet tall on average. They wear "uniforms", Baggy beige pants made of "paper-cloth" a material that feels like cloth but has paper like properties (crumples), but is very sturdy. Brown or maroon boots. a beige or light brown shirt with a darker brown vest. A maroon necktie or bow. And a red hairband that keeps the long, straightly cut hair in a bun or ponytail.

(year ~100 I) Going back to prehistory. The earliest Kenovians looked simmilar to the deathclaw from fallout, the Females were 9 feet tall while the males where about 6 feet tall. They had large lizard like tails, using them as a third leg as they were bipedal. Their arms were well developed and had 4 clawed fingers and a thumb. They were grey-orange and covered with small scales. Despite this appearance Kenovians were warm blooded mammals who gave live birth. The males had "poison glands" and could inject paralizing venom using their long "fangs". The other teeth for both genders were sharp and of equal size, a feature that still remains in modern day kenovians though the poison glands have long since lost usability. The glands in modern day are the equivelant of an apendix in humans although males stil have their hollow fangs. In this time, Kenovians were in their "stone age" and lived in small tribes that often fought with each other. Both genders would hunt and many land animals would go extinct due to early Kenovians dominance as apex predators.

(year ~200,000 I) By their "bronze age" Kenovians (Kentre) began to shift in appearance as scales were replaced with skin and their faces began to look more human than animal. It is theorized that this "evolution" was caused by outside radiation or tomfoolery by a advanced humanoid race, though this is not confirmed. In this peroid, Kenovians began to biuld towns although their social strucre was still tribal and their society stemmed from war.

(year ~300,000 I) This period of time in Kenovain history is often called "the steel age". Kenovians had almost entierly become humanoid, loosing their tails and looking almost the same as modern Kenovians. The females were now on average 8 feet tall and the males were still 6 feet tall. Kentre hair was a bright yellow that may or may not glow under a black light. They have no other hair on their body, only on their head, not even the small hairs that grow on our arms and legs are present on ither gender. The hair they do have though grows incredibly fast and they would often wrap themselves in it instaid of clothing.

During the steel age, towns were replaced by cities and "unions" were formed, leading into the first industreal age. In the steel Age, the tribes were replaced with "kingdoms", called Kerrikes (Kare-rai-kz). The Kentre would also begin to circumnavigate thir planet, exploring the tundra and desert. In the desert they met the Kensaa (Ken-sah) who they almost entierly wiped out because Kenovians were incredibly racist and xenophobic.

(year ~400,000 I) This is around the time of the first Kenovian Industreal Age, when kenovians finally discovered steam power and mass production. By this point, many of the Kerrmasts (Kare-mah-st-z), the Kenovian monarchs, were replaced with councils. Unions would form into compaines and the stock market arose. This is around the time Kenovians began to wear clothes.

(year ~450,000 I) The Heat. The Kenovian star, Kazamet (Kah-zah-met) began to slowly expand and the planet wou,d start to heat up.

(year 500,000 I)(aka. year 0 II) The birth of the catalyst. In one of the last monarchies of Kenova, a prince was born. He would become the first "dictator" of Kenova. He managed to conquer the entire Twilight Zone and would begin a policy of rapid industrealization.

Kensnna

Arising around the same time as the Kentre, the Kensnna lived in caves in the snowy tundra of Kenova. They never developed outside their stone age but during the great evacuation, they proved to be capible and even eager to sociaslize with the Kentre, seeing no differnece in the two races. The Kensnna have thicker pale skin despite having the same blood color and typeings. Their skin also emits a faint blue glow due to the glowing blue mushrooms that make up for a majority of their diet. They are about a foot taller than their Kentre relatives. They have thick black hair that grows not only on their head, but they also have armpit and pubic hair, though none have facial, limb, or nose hair. They have bright red eyes and limited night vision. They have very sharp hearing. Another sharp thing is their long claws.

Kensaa

The Kensaa are a now extinct Kenovain race who eveolved at around the same pace as the Kentre but were phiscally weaker and less populous. The males were around 4 feet tall and the females were 6 feet tall. They had short brown hair and hair on their feet. They retained some brown scales around their sides, back, and neck. They even still had tails. They had tan skin and lived nomadic and peaceful lives. As they were compatibe with other Kenovains, some Kentre had secret relationships with the Kensaa and some Kensaa genes exist even today though it is too small of a margin to cause any notable change.
